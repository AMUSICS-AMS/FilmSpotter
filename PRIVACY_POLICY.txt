# FilmSpotter Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** February 16, 2026

## 1. Introduction

FilmSpotter ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our Service.

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Information You Provide
- **User Name:** You provide a display name when creating or joining a session
- **Project Names:** Names you assign to your video projects
- **Event Markers:** Timecoded notes, markers, and annotations you create
- **Session IDs and Passwords:** Optional session passwords you create

### 2.2 Information Automatically Collected
- **Browser Information:** Browser type, version, and operating system
- **Usage Data:** How you interact with the Service (features used, session duration)
- **Session Data:** Temporary session identifiers stored in localStorage

### 2.3 Information We DO NOT Collect
- **Video Files:** We do NOT store, access, or process your video files
- **Personal Identifiable Information:** We do not require email addresses, phone numbers, or real names
- **Payment Information:** Currently not collected (free tier)

## 3. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:
- Provide and maintain the Service
- Improve user experience and Service functionality
- Monitor usage patterns to optimize performance
- Detect, prevent, and address technical issues
- Communicate with you about the Service (if contact information is provided)

## 4. Data Storage

### 4.1 Local Storage (Browser)
- Event markers, session data, and user preferences are stored in your browser's localStorage
- This data remains on your device and is NOT transmitted to our servers
- You can delete this data at any time by clearing your browser's localStorage

### 4.2 Third-Party Storage
- Video files are hosted by third-party services YOU choose (Dropbox, Vimeo, Google Drive, etc.)
- We do not have access to these files
- These services have their own privacy policies that govern how they handle your data

### 4.3 Server Storage (Future)
- If real-time collaboration features are implemented, event data may be stored on our servers
- You will be explicitly notified and asked to consent before this feature is enabled
- Server-stored data will be encrypted and accessible only to authorized session participants

## 5. Data Sharing and Disclosure

### 5.1 We DO NOT Sell Your Data
We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties.

### 5.2 We May Share Data With:
- **Service Providers:** Third-party vendors who help us operate the Service (hosting, analytics, etc.)
- **Legal Requirements:** If required by law, regulation, legal process, or governmental request
- **Protection of Rights:** To protect our rights, property, or safety, or that of our users or others

### 5.3 Collaborative Sessions
- When you join a collaborative session, your user name and markers are visible to other session participants
- Use session passwords to control who can access your sessions

## 6. Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your data, including:
- HTTPS encryption for data transmission
- Secure session IDs
- Optional password protection for sessions
- Regular security audits

However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

## 7. Data Retention

### 7.1 Local Data
- Data stored in your browser's localStorage persists until you manually clear it
- Clearing your browser data will permanently delete all local session information

### 7.2 Server Data (If Implemented)

## 8. Your Rights and Choices

### 8.1 Access and Control

- You can delete your data at any time by clearing localStorage
- You can export your data using the export feature

### 8.2 Opt-Out
- You can stop using the Service at any time
- Closing your browser or clearing localStorage will remove all local data

### 8.3 Do Not Track
Currently, we do not respond to "Do Not Track" signals because we do not track users across third-party websites.
